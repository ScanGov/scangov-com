ScanGov to support judging for 2026 Government Experience Awards

Will provide website digital experience data to the Center for Digital Government’s annual GovX Awards program.

  • July 14, 2026

ScanGov will again provide essential digital experience data to support the Center for Digital Government's 2026 Government Experience Awards (GovX) judging process, including sitewide quantitative analysis of award finalists.

The scans evaluate key government digital experience indicators based on public policy, established web protocols, guidelines, and industry best practices:

  • Accessibility
  • Botability
  • Security
  • Usability

About GovX

The GovX Awards showcase the latest trends, best practices, and ideas around the evolving experience of government and recognize the states, cities, and counties at the forefront of the government experience movement.

About the Center for Digital Government

The Center for Digital Government (CDG) is a national benchmarking and advisory institute focused on technology policy and best practices in state and local government. Learn more at www.govtech.com/cdg.

CDG is a trusted brand of e.Republic. Learn more at www.erepublic.com.

About ScanGov

ScanGov is a government digital experience monitoring, reporting and remediation service. It maintains Project ScanGov, an open source digital experience monitor that grades government websites based on key digital experience indicators. It also maintains ScanGov Standards, a publicly-available set of digital experience standards based on public policy and established web protocol, guidelines, and best practices.
