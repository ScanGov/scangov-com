GovX Awards

Supporting the Center for Digital Government's Government Experience Awards.

The Government Experience Awards celebrate innovation that directly impacts how the public engages with government. ScanGov has been instrumental in helping us identify the best digital government experiences. By incorporating ScanGovs expanded data capabilities into our evaluation process, were deepening our understanding of what defines a leading digital government experiencefrom accessibility to laying the foundation for AI, this layer of insight is invaluable in identifying the jurisdictions setting the gold standard for service delivery.
