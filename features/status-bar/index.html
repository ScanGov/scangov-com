Status bar

See how many pages still have issues out of your total, right on the dashboard and indicator pages.

What

  • Pages with issues vs. total, at a glance
  • Shown on the dashboard and every indicator page
  • Updates automatically as pages get fixed

Features

Sites dashboard

Monitor every domain you manage from one dashboard, with grades and scores at a glance.

Indicator monitoring

Track grades and scores for every indicator, then drill into the standards driving each one.

Domain dashboards

View domain performance from the top down, then drill into individual indicators.

Tasklist

Prioritize fixes by their impact, so your team works on what matters most.

Pages

Pinpoint exactly which pages have issues and how many, broken down by indicator.

Issue reports

Generate pre-populated issue reports your developers can act on immediately.

Download issues

Export every issue as CSV, JSON, or Markdown for spreadsheets, project boards, or AI coding agents.

Scorecards

Publish a public scorecard that shows your digital experience grades and builds trust.

Readability

Measure how easy your content is to read, using established readability standards.

Feedback

Collect public feedback and respond in the open, building accountability into your process.

Single sign-on

Let your team sign in with your organization's existing identity provider, so access stays centrally managed.

URL management

Control exactly which pages are monitored, and keep your page list in sync as your site changes.

GitHub integration

Connect your GitHub repo so issues flow straight into your team's existing developer workflow.

Scan management

Keep your monitoring data fresh with on-demand rescans and full visibility into scan status.
